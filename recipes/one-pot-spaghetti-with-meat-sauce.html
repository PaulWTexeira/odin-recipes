One Pot Spaghetti with Meat Sauce




Description

One Pot Spaghetti with Meat Sauce is a delicious and satisfying meal that combines savory ground Italian sausage with a rich tomato-basil pasta sauce. Cooked all in one pot, this dish features tender spaghetti noodles simmered to perfection, absorbing the robust flavors of the sauce. With a touch of Italian seasoning, it delivers a comforting experience, perfect for any meal.

Explore the steps and ingredients below to see how to bring this hearty meal to your table.



Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground Italian sausage

  • 1 small white onion, diced

  • 4 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1 (26 ounce) jar tomato-basil pasta sauce

  • 2 cups water

  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning

  • 1 (8 ounce) package spaghetti noodles, broken in half

  • Â½ cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese for serving (Optional)


Steps


  1. Combine ground sausage, onions, and garlic in a large pot or skillet with tall sides. Cook over medium heat until sausage is cooked through, 5 to 8 minutes. Drain and discard grease.


  2. Stir pasta sauce, water, and Italian seasoning into the pot; bring to a boil. Stir in spaghetti noodles, return to a boil, and cook, stirring occasionally, until noodles are cooked through and sauce has thickened, 17 to 20 minutes.


  3. Serve topped with grated Parmesan cheese.
